### question
  A cyclist travels at [v] m/s for [t] seconds.
  How far does the cyclist travel?
  
### parameters
  v = random.randint(2, 7)  
  t = 7
  
### solution
  - v+t  
  - v/t  
  - **v*t**  
  - v-t

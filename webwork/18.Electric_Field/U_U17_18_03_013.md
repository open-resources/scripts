## Question Section 

At what distance is the electrostatic force between two protons equal to the weight of one proton?
ans_rule(40) m


## Answer Section

g = 9.80
q1 = (1.6)*(10**-19)
q = q1**2
mp = (1.67)*(10**-27)
k = 8.99*(10**9)
a = (k*q)/(g*mp)
r = (a)**(1/2)

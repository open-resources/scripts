## Question Section 

Two point charges exert a(F1) N force on each other. What will the force become if the distance between them is increased by a factor of three?
ans_rule(40) N



## Answer Section

from random import randrange
F1 = randrange(1.02,19.99,1.73)
b = 1/3
r = b**2
F2 = F1*r

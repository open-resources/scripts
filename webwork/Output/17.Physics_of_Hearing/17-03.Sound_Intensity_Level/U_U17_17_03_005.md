## Question Section 

What sound intensity level in(dB) is produced by earphones that create an intensity of(4.00times 10^-2Wm^2)?



## Answer Section

showHint = 3

I = 4*(10**-2)
Io = 1*(10**-12)
dB = 10*log10(I/Io)

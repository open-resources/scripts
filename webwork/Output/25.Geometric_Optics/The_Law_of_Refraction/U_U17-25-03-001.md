What is the speed of light in water? In glycerine? The indices of refraction for water is(1.333) and for glycerine is(1.473).
Water =ans_rule(40)(ms)
Glycerine =ans_rule(40)(ms)



## Answer Section

speed_waterSI = (c)/(1.333)

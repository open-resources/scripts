## Answer Section

P = 1.013*10**5
Na = 6.022*10**23
R = 8.314
T = 273.15

NVm = ((P)*(Na))/((R)*(T))
NVum = (NVm)/(10**18)
distanceum = (NVum)**(-1/3)

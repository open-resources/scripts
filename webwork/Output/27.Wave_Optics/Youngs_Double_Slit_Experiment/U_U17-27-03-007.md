## Question Section 

At what angle is the fourth-order maximum for(450,(nm) wavelength blue light falling on double slits separated by(0.0500,(mm)?



## Answer Section

P1 = 4*4.5*(10**(-7))/(5*(10**(-5)))
A1 = asin(P1)
